Aesthetics & Marketing

Originally comprised of two separate departments, the Aesthetics and Marketing team work as one. Aesthetics members are responsible for the appearance of the team, both related to the robot and the team in public. This includes organizing community outreach events, recruiting new members, designing our signature green tent, and any art related project needed to be done. On top of this, they manage the pit area, scouting and awards submissions. Aesthetics members learn how to use graphic design software such as Photoshop, as well as basic CAD.

Marketing is the department responsible for getting enough funds every single year to build the robot. Members can expect to give professional presentations, create a business plan and then follow it, and manage money effectively. They work closely with the Aesthetics team to provide a good public outlook for our team. Our Treasurer is a subset in the Marketing department.

Andrew is in charge of both Aesthetics and Marketing, and somehow manages not to go insane.
