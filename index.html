We are currently in place at the Ellensburg District Competition!

Come check out our blog for detailed updates.

We are FIRST Robotics Team 2605, the Sehome Seamonsters! We work out of the Robotics Room provided to us by Sehome High School in Bellingham, Washington.

If you’re not already familiar with FIRST Robotics, it’s probably a good idea to head over to our FIRST Information page.

Our Team at a Glance...

Full Team Name: The Sehome Seamonsters Robotics Team

Official Team Sponsors: NASA, Bellingham School District, Platt Electric, Lund Engineering, OSPI & Sehome High School

Team Number: 2605

Location: Sehome High School, Bellingham, WA

Established: 2008

Mascot Name: Queequeg

Team Colors: Green, Black, Gold
